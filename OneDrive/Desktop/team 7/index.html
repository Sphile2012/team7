💡
NOMSA'S USER PROFILE
📍

Who She Is

Nomsa, 32, owner of a food truck business in Johannesburg. Passionate, driven but overwhelmed by manual processes.

😤

Pain Points

Runs out of stock unexpectedly. Loses track of sales. Spends hours doing admin. No real-time insights.

🎯

Her Goal

To manage her inventory, payments and business analytics from one simple, affordable mobile app.

💬

Her Words

"I just need to know what I have, what I sold and what I made — without spending hours on spreadsheets."

🔍
THE IDENTIFIED
PROBLEM

Small and medium enterprises (SMEs) like food truck operators lack an integrated, affordable digital tool to manage inventory, payments, and sales analytics in real time — forcing them to rely on fragmented, manual processes that cost time and revenue.

HOW MIGHT WE
📦

Inventory

How might we help Nomsa track stock levels in real time without manual counting?

💳

Payments

How might we simplify payment collection so Nomsa never loses a sale?

📊

Analytics

How might we give Nomsa clear insights into her business performance daily?
